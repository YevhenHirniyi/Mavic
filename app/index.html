What is the Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel ideal for aerial photography, featuring all the best DJI technology to transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. L1D-20c camera powered by unique Hasselblad technology Natural Color Solution (HNCS) 5 allowing users to make stunning aerial shots with 20 megapixels and stunning colors.
